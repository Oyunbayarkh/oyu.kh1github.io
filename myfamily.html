My family

Family is everything,

And all we need is each other.

Here you can see my family. Ours is a family with four members in it. Dad, mum, brother and me. My father is a veterinarian. But he works as a teacher for a driving training company. And my mum is an English teacher. She works at a school in our province.My brother and I are both university students.

Our parents, like other Mongolian families, teach us to respect Mongolian traditions and parents and the elderly. I think they are very good at teaching and setting an example for us, because they are both teachers.
